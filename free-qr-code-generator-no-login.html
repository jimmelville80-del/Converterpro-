What is a QR Code?

A QR (Quick Response) code is a scannable barcode that links to digital content. Customers point their phone camera at the code, and it instantly opens a URL, WiFi network, business card, or plain text. QR codes are perfect for marketing, contact sharing, and bridging offline-to-online experiences.

Why Use QR Codes?

  • No download needed: Modern phones scan QR codes natively with the camera app — no special app required.
  • Works for everything: URLs, WiFi passwords, vCards (digital business cards), text, email addresses, and phone numbers.
  • Perfect for marketing: Link print ads, posters, and packaging to landing pages, social media, or special offers.
  • Share WiFi instantly: Generate a QR code for your WiFi network and guests can connect by scanning.
  • Digital business cards: Create a vCard QR code to share contact info instead of printing cards.
