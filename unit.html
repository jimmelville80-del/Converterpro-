Frequently Asked Questions

What units can I convert?

Unit Converter Pro handles length, weight, temperature, volume, speed, area, pressure, energy, and 50+ other measurement categories.

How do I convert km to miles?

Select Length as the category, enter your value in kilometres, and the converter instantly shows the equivalent in miles (1 km = 0.621 miles).

How do I convert Celsius to Fahrenheit?

Select Temperature, enter your value in Celsius, and get the Fahrenheit equivalent instantly. Formula: °F = (°C × 9/5) + 32.

Is the unit converter free?

Yes, completely free with no signup required.

Can I convert kg to lbs?

Yes. Select Weight, enter your value in kilograms, and get the result in pounds instantly. 1 kg = 2.205 lbs.
