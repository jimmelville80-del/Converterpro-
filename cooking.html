How to Convert Cups to Grams

Converting between cups, grams, mL and ounces is essential for accurate baking. Unlike cooking, baking is a science — small measurement errors can ruin a recipe.

The problem with measuring by cups is that every ingredient has a different density. A cup of almond flour weighs 112g, while a cup of honey weighs 340g — nearly three times heavier in the same measuring cup.

Why Grams Are Better

Professional bakers always measure by weight. It's faster (no measuring cups to wash), more accurate, and gives consistent results every time.

US vs Australian Cups

A US cup = 236.6mL. An Australian cup = 250mL. Most online recipes use US cups. When in doubt, use our converter and weigh your ingredients.

Common Conversions

  • 1 cup almond flour = 112g
  • 1 cup plain flour = 125g
  • 1 cup white sugar = 200g
  • 1 cup butter = 227g
  • 1 cup honey = 340g
  • 1 cup milk = 240g
