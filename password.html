Frequently Asked Questions

Are my generated passwords safe?

Yes. All passwords are generated entirely in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server or stored anywhere.

How long should a secure password be?

Security experts recommend at least 12 characters. For high-security accounts, use 16-20+ characters with a mix of letters, numbers and symbols.

Can I generate multiple passwords at once?

Yes. Use the bulk generation option to create multiple strong passwords at once.

What makes a password strong?

A strong password is long (12+ characters), uses uppercase and lowercase letters, includes numbers and special symbols, and is unique to each account.
