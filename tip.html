Frequently Asked Questions

How do I calculate a tip?

Enter the bill total, select your tip percentage (15%, 18%, 20% or custom), and the calculator shows your tip amount and total instantly.

How do I split a bill between multiple people?

Enter the bill total, choose your tip percentage, then enter the number of people. The calculator shows each person's share including tip.

What is a standard tip percentage?

In Australia, tipping is optional but 10-15% is common for excellent service. In the US, 15-20% is standard for restaurants.

Is the tip calculator free?

Yes, completely free with no signup required.

Can I use a custom tip percentage?

Yes. Enter any tip percentage you want, not just the preset options.
